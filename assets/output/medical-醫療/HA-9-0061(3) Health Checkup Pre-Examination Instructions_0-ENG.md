If you have acute fever (forehead temperature over 37.5°C, re-measure ear temperature; if ear temperature exceeds 38°C), respiratory symptoms, diarrhea, cough, taste symptoms, or other physical discomforts, please seek medical attention promptly and postpone the health checkup.

## 1. General Precautions

1. Eat a light diet 3 days before the checkup.

2. If the checkup includes functional medicine tests, stop taking vitamins the day before the checkup; women are not advised to test estrogen metabolism during menstruation and may return to the health checkup center within 7 days after the period ends for a supplementary test.

3. The day before the checkup:

(1). Stool sample: Collect the sample according to the stool sample collection instructions. After collection, place it in the green plastic bag and keep it in a cool place.

(2). Starting from midnight, fasting and no water intake (including any fluids) are required.

4. Please arrive at the E-Da Hospital Health Checkup Center on the 6th floor at ___ year ___ month ___ day, at ___ o'clock, with your ID card, National Health Insurance card, stool sample box, various consent forms, etc., ready for check-in.

## Medication Precautions

1. For those who take long-term diabetes medication, please do not take it on the morning of the checkup day and bring the medication to the health checkup center to take after completing fasting tests.

2. For those who take long-term chronic disease medications (such as for hypertension, heart disease, and thyroid medications), please follow your doctor's instructions and bring the medications to the health checkup center.
