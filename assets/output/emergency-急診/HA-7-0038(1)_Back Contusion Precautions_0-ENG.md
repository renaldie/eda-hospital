# Precautions for Back Contusion

1. Try to lie flat and rest more.

2. Avoid bending over to lift heavy objects.

3. When moving, you can support the painful area with your hand to reduce pain.

4. Ice the area within 2 days after injury to reduce swelling and relieve pain.

(1) Put an appropriate amount of ice cubes into an ice bag and add water up to one-third.

(2) Turn the ice bag upside down to check for leaks, wrap the ice bag with a dry towel, and apply it to the affected area.

(3) Ice the area four times a day, each time for 15-20 minutes, then rest.

5. Take medication as prescribed by the doctor and return for regular follow-up examinations.

6. If you experience blood in urine, back pain accompanied by cold sweats, please go to the emergency room immediately.
