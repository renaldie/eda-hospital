# Hypoglycemia Care

## What is hypoglycemia?

Hypoglycemia usually refers to a blood glucose concentration below 70 mg/dl, which can easily cause symptoms of hypoglycemia. Therefore, when blood glucose is below 70 mg/dl, the possibility of hypoglycemia should be noted.

## Why does hypoglycemia occur?

1. Not eating on time after injection or medication

2. Overuse of oral medication or insulin

3. Sudden increase in exercise without proper snack supplementation

4. Fasting for too long without eating
