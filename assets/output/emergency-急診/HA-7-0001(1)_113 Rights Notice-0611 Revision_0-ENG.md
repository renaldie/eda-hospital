## Rights and Information for Victims of Domestic Violence

When you suffer from domestic violence and feel scared, helpless, or sad, not knowing what to do, you can call the 24-hour toll-free Domestic Violence Prevention and Protection Hotline 113. The Domestic Violence and Sexual Assault Prevention Center has professionally trained staff to provide you with necessary assistance and consultation, and will keep your personal information and requests for help confidential.
