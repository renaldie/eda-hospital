## Low-Fat Diet Health Education

1. Applicable to: high blood lipids, cholecystitis, gallstones, severe diarrhea, cardiovascular diseases.

2. Purpose: to limit the intake of dietary fats in order to reduce the burden on the pancreas, intestines, gallbladder, and cardiovascular system.

3. Food categories: skim milk, lean meat, liver, heart, kidneys, non-fried bean products, fish roe, oysters, shrimp, eel, abalone, octopus, sardines, scallops, squid, etc.

4. Cooking methods that do not require added oil, such as steaming, boiling, stewing, braising, roasting, red-cooking, and cold mixing, can be used to enhance food texture, along with mild seasonings to stimulate appetite.
