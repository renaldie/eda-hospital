## NOTE :

1. This test is not applicable to female during the menstrual period or if you are having bleeding from hemorrhoids.

## Procedures :

1. Urinate at the start time but DO NOT collect this urine. Then collect all urine sample to the container for the following 24 hours. The container should be kept at  $ 4^{\circ} $ C.

2. When finished collecting, measure the total volume of the urine (the amount of c.c.), then write down on the test tube (No.1).

3. Mix the urine well and draw 10 c.c. into the test tube (No.1).

4. If the container is full before the end of collection period, please write down the volume and draw 10 c.c. into the test tube (No.1). Then discard the urine in the container.

5. Continue collecting the urine until 24 hours, then draw another 10 c.c. of urine into the test tube (No.2). Please sum up the urine volume and write down on the test tube (No.2).

6. Take the test tubes (with ice bath) to the window of 2F phlebotomy room. Confirm the total volume of 24 hours urine with our staffs.

7. When CCR test is performed, please hand in your sample in person for additional blood test.
