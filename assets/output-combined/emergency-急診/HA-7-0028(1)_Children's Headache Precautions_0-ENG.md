## Precautions for Children's Headaches

1. Rest in bed more after returning home, elevate the head of the bed, maintain adequate sleep, and take medication as directed by the doctor.

2. Try to reduce life stress and relax.

3. Return for a follow-up visit if any of the following occur: blurred vision or double vision, weakness or numbness or tingling in one side of the body, neck stiffness, persistent vomiting, or altered consciousness. There are many causes of headaches; it is recommended to return to the clinic for further follow-up.
