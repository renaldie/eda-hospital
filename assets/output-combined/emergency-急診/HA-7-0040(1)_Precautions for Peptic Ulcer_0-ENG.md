## Precautions for Peptic Ulcers

1. Try to relax while eating, chew slowly and thoroughly, and rest briefly after meals before starting work.

2. Have three meals at regular times and in fixed amounts.

3. Avoid smoking, alcohol, cola, caffeine, and irritating foods.

4. Maintain a regular lifestyle, avoid staying up late, and keep a happy mood.

5. Regular outpatient follow-up and control.

6. If experiencing severe abdominal pain, pain shifting to the lower right abdomen, high fever, or persistent black stools, seek medical attention promptly.
