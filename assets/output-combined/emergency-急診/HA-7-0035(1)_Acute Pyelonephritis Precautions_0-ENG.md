## Precautions for Acute Pyelonephritis

1. If there are no contraindications, drink more than 2000~3000ml of water daily.

2. Pay attention to perineal hygiene, urinate every 1~2 hours, and do not hold urine.

3. Follow medication instructions, rest in bed during the acute phase, and avoid consuming irritating foods, tobacco, and alcohol.

4. Continue outpatient follow-up and examinations after the acute phase, do not stop medication on your own to prevent relapse.

5. If experiencing high fever with chills or severe flank pain, seek medical attention promptly.
