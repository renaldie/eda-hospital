## 3. Collection Steps:

1. Defecate into a squat toilet, bedpan, clean garbage bag, or paper. Do not mix with urine, toilet water, or toilet paper to avoid contamination.

2. Unscrew the bottle cap and use the plastic stick to scoop the stool. Please select stool containing blood, pus, or mucus, place it into the bottle, and tighten the cap.

3. For watery or loose stool, use a plastic straw to suck about 2 c.c. of stool into the bottle.

## 4. Post-Collection Precautions:

1. Stool routine examination and occult blood test [chemical method] (OB [Chemical]): Deliver the specimen at room temperature within 4 hours after collection. If it cannot be delivered the same day, refrigerate first and send for testing within 24 hours.

2. Parasite egg concentration method (Parasite OVA (Concentrated method)): Deliver the specimen at room temperature within 1 hour after collection.
