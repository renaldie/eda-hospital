Explanation: Drinking rose tea during menstruation can help alleviate symptoms.

(2) Rose Tea:

Ingredients: 10 grams of rose petals, an appropriate amount of rock sugar.

Preparation: Brew with boiling water as a tea drink.

Explanation: Warms the meridians, relieves depression, disperses liver stagnation, and regulates qi; suitable for women's daily health maintenance.
