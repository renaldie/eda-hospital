(Three) Five-nut Porridge: 30 grams each of sesame seeds, pine nut, walnut, peach kernel (with skin and tip removed), and sweet apricot kernel (with heart removed), and 100 grams of japonica rice.

Preparation: Mix all the above ingredients and grind them into a powder using a grinder. Then add the rice and cook into porridge.

(Four) White Mushroom Tea: Boil 300 grams of cleaned white mushrooms with 1000–1500 mL of water. Add a suitable amount of rock sugar according to personal preference.
