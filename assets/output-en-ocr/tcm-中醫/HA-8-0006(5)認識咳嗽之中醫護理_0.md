Complete, it is not recommended to feed honey, as it may expose you to the risk of fish meat toxin bacteria.

After severe coughing, the vocal cords may be affected, leading to a hoarse voice. You can purchase 3 grams of pu gai and half a lotus fruit, then stew them with honey, which helps reduce inflammation and restore your voice.
