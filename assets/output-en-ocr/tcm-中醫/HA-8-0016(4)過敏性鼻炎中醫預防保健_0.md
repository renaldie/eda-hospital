(二) Hegu point: the depression between the thumb and index finger, in the area of the "虎口" (the hollow of the wrist); it can calm the nerves, relieve pain, and enhance resistance.

(三) Zusanli point: located about four finger-widths below the outer depression of the knee, it can harmonize the stomach and intestines and enhance resistance.
