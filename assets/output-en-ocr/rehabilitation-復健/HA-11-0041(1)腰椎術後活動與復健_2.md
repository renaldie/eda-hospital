## Post-lumbar surgery rehabilitation exercises

Exercise purpose: After surgery, performing rehabilitation exercises in coordination can improve surgical success rates and reduce the recurrence of back pain.

Exercise prescription: Hold for 10~15 seconds, then relax. Perform 15 repetitions daily, four times a day.
