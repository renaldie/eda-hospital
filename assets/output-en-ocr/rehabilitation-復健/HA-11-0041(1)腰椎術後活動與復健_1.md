## Post-lumbar surgery activity techniques

Purpose of activity: Correct positioning movements can help alleviate post-surgical pain.

Notes:  
(1) When lying flat and resting, you may not need to wear a back brace. Place pillows under the knees and calves;  
When lying on your side, slightly bend both legs and place pillows between them to reduce pressure on the lower back.

(2) When engaging in activities or exercise, you must wear the back brace correctly to protect your surgical site.  
(3) The time when you do not need to wear the back brace for protection should follow your doctor's instructions.

Turning over: Place the opposite foot on the bed, then turn the entire body together (maintain a straight spine).

Sitting to standing:  
(1) Put on the back brace and shift your buttocks forward  
(2) Use hands to support the handrail  
(3) Stand up
