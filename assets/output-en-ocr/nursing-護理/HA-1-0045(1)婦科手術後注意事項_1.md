## ___ Lady

## 1. Precautions:

For patients undergoing general anesthesia, after full recovery and consciousness, food intake can be resumed. Begin by sipping small amounts of liquid (such as plain water, sugar water, milk, or juice). If there is no nausea or vomiting, proceed to consume liquid foods such as milk porridge or clear porridge.

☐ A sponge pad is placed inside the vagina. Please remove it yourself at ___ o'clock ___ minutes in the morning and afternoon after returning home to prevent infection. Ensure the number of sponges matches the original count.

☐ There will be slight vaginal bleeding after surgery, which will appear on the sponge pad as large as a 5-cent or 10-cent coin. This is a normal phenomenon.

☐ During the first week, take a shower only. Avoid sitz baths or vaginal irrigation.

☐ Abstain from sexual activity for 4 weeks after surgery.

☐ If any of the following symptoms occur, please go to the emergency department immediately:

1. Abdominal pain not normally experienced  
2. Heavy vaginal bleeding (soaking a whole sanitary pad)  
3. Chills or fever above 38°C  
4. Foul-smelling vaginal discharge
