## Dementia - Care Considerations

Dementia is not a normal part of aging. It results from degeneration of the central nervous system, brain injury, cerebrovascular disease, or illness. Symptoms primarily include cognitive impairment, short-term memory loss, and loss of daily living abilities. It may also present with disruptive behaviors, personality changes, delusions, or hallucinations.
