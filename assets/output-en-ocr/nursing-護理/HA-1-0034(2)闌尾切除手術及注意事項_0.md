Four. Post-discharge precautions:

1. Avoid lifting heavy objects or engaging in intense physical activities for the first six weeks after surgery to prevent wound dehiscence.

2. When changing dressings daily, observe for signs of infection such as fever, redness, swelling, abnormal bleeding, purulent discharge, or a foul odor at the wound site. If any of these symptoms occur, seek medical attention and return for follow-up examination immediately.
