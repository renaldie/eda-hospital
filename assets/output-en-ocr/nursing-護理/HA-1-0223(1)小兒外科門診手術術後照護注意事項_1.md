## Five. Important Notes:

1. If there is a slight fever (below 38°C) or drowsiness on the day of surgery, this is a normal postoperative reaction and there is no need to panic. Most patients recover to normal within a few hours.

2. If any of the following symptoms occur, please go to the emergency department immediately:

(1) High fever with chills or nausea and vomiting.

(2) Excessive bleeding from the wound.

(3) Abdominal pain that is unusual or severe.
