## [11. Outpatient Schedule]

### 1. Collecting Total Parenteral Nutrition (TPN)

Visit the outpatient clinic of the nutrition specialist physician every week for blood tests. The physician and specialized venous nurse will assess the home use of parenteral nutrition. The physician will decide, based on the situation, whether to schedule follow-up visits at regular intervals.
