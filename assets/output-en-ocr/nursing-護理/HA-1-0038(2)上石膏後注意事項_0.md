## Four. If any of the following symptoms occur after discharge, you should visit the clinic immediately:

1. Worsening limb pain or swelling.

2. Persistent numbness or difficulty moving the limb.

3. Nail (or toenail) turning blue-purple or remaining pale even after local pressure for several seconds.

4. Discomfort inside the cast or cracking of the cast.

5. Any foul odor or leakage from the cast.
