## Management of dialysate and other waste from peritoneal dialysis

## I. Applicable population: Peritoneal dialysis patients

## II. Handling principles:

## (1) At home:

1. Cut open the dialysate collection bag and pour the dialysate down the toilet to flush it away.

2. The dialysate soft bag and used catheter sets should be disposed of in a household general trash bin, treated as regular household waste.

## (2) In the hospital:

Dialysate soft bags, machine tubing sets, and white caps, as they have come into contact with body fluids, are considered infectious waste and should be disposed of in red infectious waste bins.
