## Grief Care

After a loved one passes away, you or your family may experience the following reactions:

1. Accepting the reality and emotions of loss: You may feel confused due to loss, find it difficult to concentrate, want or actually cry intensely, feel uneasy, sad, helpless, painful, abandoned, angry, feel that the situation is unreal, have difficulty controlling yourself, lack of security, fear, anxiety, search for the deceased, reminisce about the deceased, miss the deceased, feel as if the deceased still exists, hear the deceased's voice or see hallucinations, have trouble sleeping due to nightmares.

2. Physical discomfort: numbness, throat tightness, shortness of breath, chest tightness, nausea, weakness, digestive issues, insomnia, unstable blood pressure, blurred vision, headaches, weight loss, weakened immunity, hormonal imbalance, lack of energy, emptiness, diarrhea or constipation, dizziness, etc.
