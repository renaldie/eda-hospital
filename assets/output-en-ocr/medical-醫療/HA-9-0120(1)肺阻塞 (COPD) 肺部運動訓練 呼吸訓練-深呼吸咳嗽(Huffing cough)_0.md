## Importance of Breathing Exercises

1. Chronic respiratory inflammation leads to airway obstruction, potentially causing dynamic hyperinflation in the lungs and limiting patients' physical activity capacity.  
2. Breathing exercises can increase exhalation time, reducing gas retention in the lungs and helping to decrease dynamic hyperinflation during exercise, thereby improving patients' breathlessness.
