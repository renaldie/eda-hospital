## Endoscopic Variceal Ligation Precautions

### 2. When food intake is allowed:

(1) For the first 1~3 days, consume liquid diets such as fruit juice, rice milk, soy milk, milk, and rice porridge.

(2) From day 4 to 7, transition to soft diets such as porridge and noodles.

(3) After one week, normal diet can be resumed.

3. After returning home, if any signs of bleeding occur (e.g., coffee-ground vomit, black stools), please contact us during working hours (Monday to Friday, 8:00–17:00). After working hours, please go to the emergency department.
