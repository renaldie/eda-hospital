## ☑ Manual Functions

1. Effectively establish a standardized process for recording dialysis procedures to facilitate effective communication among various departments.

2. Establish standardized operating procedures for surgical treatments.

3. Assist dialysis patients in understanding their own conditions and managing their health status.

4. Improve the patient-doctor relationship and enhance the quality of medical care.

## ☑ Usage Timing

1. Issued by the dialysis department at the beginning of dialysis treatment.

2. Carried to the relevant department during any procedures involving dialysis catheter surgery to facilitate the execution of related operations.

3. The relevant surgical departments shall record in detail the surgical procedures (including diagrams) for easy reference.

4. During dialysis, patients should hand over the records to the dialysis department for storage; when transferring to another dialysis center, the records should be handed over to the patient to carry and continue use.
