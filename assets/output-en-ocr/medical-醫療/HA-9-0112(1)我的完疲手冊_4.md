## Fatigue: Take Small Steps Forward

## Using Exercise to Alleviate Cancer-Related Fatigue

<div style="text-align: center;"><img src="imgs/img_in_image_box_76_364_173_489.jpg" alt="Image" width="5%" /></div>

|  | Exercise Type | Exercise Intensity (Perceived) |
|---|---|---|
| Warm-up period<br>5–10 minutes | Slow walking | Perceived exertion < 8<br>(Very easy) |
| Training period<br>15–20 minutes | Moderate-speed walking | Perceived exertion ≤ 13<br>(A bit strenuous, still able to talk) |
| Cool-down period<br>5–10 minutes | Slow walking | Perceived exertion < 8<br>(Very easy) |
