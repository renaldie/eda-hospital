# Oral Glucose Tolerance Test (50/75/100g) Collection and Health Education Sheet

1. Oral 50g glucose tolerance test:

1.1 This test does not require fasting.

1.2 Go to the registration counter to collect 5 bottles of glucose solution (each bottle 50% concentration, 20mL).

1.3 Drink all 5 bottles within 5 minutes. After drinking the glucose solution, do not eat anything; you may drink plain water only.

1.4 Blood draw is required at 60 minutes after consumption.

2. Oral 75g glucose tolerance test:

1.1 Fasting for 8 hours is required. A blood sample should be drawn before fasting.

1.2 Go to the registration counter to collect 8 bottles of glucose solution (each bottle 50% concentration, 20mL).

1.3 Drink 7.5 bottles of glucose solution within 5 minutes. After drinking the glucose solution, do not eat anything; you may drink plain water only.

1.4 After drinking the glucose solution, please go to the registration counter to record the time of drinking the glucose solution and each blood draw time on the test form.

1.5 Blood draws are required at 30, 60, 90, and 120 minutes after consumption.

### 3. Oral 100g glucose tolerance test:

1.1 Fasting for 8 hours is required. A blood sample should be drawn before fasting.

1.2 Go to the registration counter to collect 10 bottles of glucose solution (each bottle 50% concentration, 20mL).

1.3 Drink all 10 bottles of glucose solution within 5 minutes. After drinking the glucose solution, do not eat anything; you may drink plain water only.

1.4 After drinking the glucose solution, please go to the registration counter to record the time of drinking the glucose solution and each blood draw time on the test form.

1.5 Blood draws are required at 30, 60, 120, and 180 minutes after consumption.
