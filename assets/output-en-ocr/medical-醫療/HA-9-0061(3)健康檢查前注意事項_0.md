Questionnaire Guidelines

If you have acute symptoms such as fever (axillary temperature exceeding 38°C), respiratory symptoms, diarrhea, cough, or taste disturbances, please seek medical attention immediately and reschedule your health check-up.

## General Precautions

1. Maintain a light diet for three days prior to the examination.

2. For individuals undergoing functional medicine tests, discontinue vitamin supplements the day before the examination. Women are advised not to undergo estrogen metabolism testing during menstruation; instead, they may reschedule their test for within seven days after their period ends.

3. The day before the examination:

(1) Stool sample: Collect the sample according to the stool collection instructions. After collection, place the sample in a green plastic bag and store it in a cool, shaded area.

(2) Begin fasting and water restriction (including all fluid intake) after 12:00 PM.

4. Please arrive at the Yida Hospital, 6th floor Health Examination Center, at ___ year ___ month ___ day at ___ o'clock, with your identity card, health insurance card, stool sample container, and various consent forms.

## Medication Precautions

1. Individuals taking long-term medications such as diabetes drugs should temporarily discontinue use on the day of the examination. Bring the medication to the health examination center and take it after completing the fasting-related tests.

2. Patients on long-term chronic medications (such as hypertension, cardiovascular diseases, or thyroid medications) should continue taking their prescribed medications as directed by their physician and bring the medications to the health examination center.
