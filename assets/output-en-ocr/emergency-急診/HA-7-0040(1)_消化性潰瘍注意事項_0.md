## Precautions for Peptic Ulcers

1. Eat slowly and chew food thoroughly; take a short rest after meals before resuming work.

2. Eat three balanced meals at regular times and in fixed quantities.

3. Avoid smoking, alcohol, cola, caffeine, and other stimulating foods.

4. Maintain a regular lifestyle, avoid staying up late, and keep a cheerful mood.

5. Regular outpatient follow-up and monitoring.

6. Seek medical attention promptly if experiencing severe abdominal pain, pain shifting to the lower right abdomen, high fever, or persistent passage of black, tarry stools.
