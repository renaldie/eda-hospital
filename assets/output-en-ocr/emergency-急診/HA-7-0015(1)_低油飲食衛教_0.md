## Low-fat dietary education

1. Applicable population: Elevated lipid levels, cholecystitis, gallstones, severe diarrhea, cardiovascular diseases.

2. Purpose: Limit fat intake in the diet to reduce the burden on the pancreas, gastrointestinal tract, gallbladder, and cardiovascular system.

3. Food categories: Skim milk, lean meat, liver, heart, kidneys, non-fried soy products, fish roe, oysters, shrimp, eel, abalone, octopus, sardines, dried scallops, squid, etc.

4. Cooking methods: Use cooking techniques such as steaming, boiling, stewing, braising, roasting, red-braising, and cold-dressing without adding oil to enhance food flavor, and use non-irritating seasonings to stimulate appetite.
