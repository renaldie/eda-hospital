Fruits with high sugar content should be consumed in moderation:

Pineapple (6/10 full, 1/5 fruit; 110g), Mango (6/10 full; 150g), Banana (1/2 length, 70g), Plantain (1 length)

Litchi (7 pieces, 100g), Honeydew (1/5 fruit; 210g), Lychee (13 pieces; with shell 130g, without shell 90g)

Persimmon (3/4 fruit; 70g), Pineapple sap, Rambutan (1/4 bowl / 1/2 piece; 60g)

Avoid consuming: Durian (shell removed; 1/4 segment; 45g), Avocado (1/2 bowl; 75g), Sugarcane juice, Coconut water
