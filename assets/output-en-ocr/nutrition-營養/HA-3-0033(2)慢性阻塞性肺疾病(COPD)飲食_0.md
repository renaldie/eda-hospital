Common feeding issues and improvement methods in patients with chronic obstructive pulmonary disease (COPD)

(1) Cut vegetables or meat into small, fine pieces and extend cooking time to increase intake.

(2) Food selection principles

a. Vegetables: Choose soft types of gourds (e.g., winter melon, large summer squash) or tender leaves (remove stems, select coarse leaves).

b. Fish, eggs, meat, and soy products: Choose softer fish meat, minced meat, eggs, or soy-based foods.
