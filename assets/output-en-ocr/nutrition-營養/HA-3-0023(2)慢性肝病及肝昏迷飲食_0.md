(3) Hepatic encephalopathy with coma:

① Immediate diagnosis and removal of the underlying cause; active pharmacological treatment is superior to inappropriate protein restriction.

② Recommended protein intake: 0.6–0.8 grams per kilogram of dry body weight; ensure adequate caloric intake, increasing carbohydrate and lipid sources for energy; after recovery of consciousness, gradually return to a balanced diet of 1–1.2 grams per kilogram of dry body weight.

③ Avoid foods high in ammonia (ammonia-producing foods), such as sausages, ham, fish with bones, cheese, peanuts, and foods with tough skin.
