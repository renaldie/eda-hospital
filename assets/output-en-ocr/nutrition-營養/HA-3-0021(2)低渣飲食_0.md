## Food Selection Chart


| Food Category | Foods to Avoid |
|--------------|----------------|
| Dairy and dairy products | Various dairy and dairy products, such as yogurt, kefir, dairy ice cream, cheese slices, baked goods. |
| Meat and fish | Meat and fish with skin, tendons, fried or pan-fried; chicken gizzard, duck gizzard, beef tendon, etc. High-fat meats such as animal skin, five-layer pork, pig skin, pig feet, beef shank, sausages, bacon, hot dogs, etc. |
| Egg products | Fried eggs, scrambled eggs, hard-boiled eggs that have been preserved for too long. |
| Staple foods | Whole grains and their products: rice bran, brown rice, whole grain rice, wheat bran, oatmeal, black bread, bran bread, whole wheat bread, etc. Root and stem vegetables: corn, yam, sweet potato, yam, coix seed, lotus seed, red bean, green bean, etc. |
| Vegetables | Coarse fiber vegetables: stems and old leaves of vegetables, such as celery, bamboo shoots, burdock. Raw vegetables or foods that easily produce gas: bok choy, white cabbage, broccoli, leeks, green peppers, onions, asparagus. |
| Fruits | Unfiltered fruit juice and high-fiber fruits, such as pineapple, kiwi, papaya, jujube, persimmon, rambutan, star fruit, jujube, black jujube, etc. Fruit jams and fruit paste with fruit pieces. |
| Fats | Nuts, such as cashews, sesame seeds, peanuts, walnuts, almonds, hazelnuts, chestnuts, etc. |
