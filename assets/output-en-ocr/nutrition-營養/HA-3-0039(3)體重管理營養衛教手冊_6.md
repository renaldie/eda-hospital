# Soft Diet - <3>

___ month ___ day,星期___

Recommended to eat small meals frequently, with food choices based on soft, easily digestible textures.

Recommended foods: porridge, noodles, eggs, tofu, fish, tender meat, leafy and gourd vegetables, soft fruits (pineapple, papaya, dragon fruit), low-fat milk, unsweetened soy milk.

Each meal: 1/3 to 1/2 bowl, approximately 50–200 c.c., 3–5 times per day.

● White rice should be consumed one month after surgery.

● After eating, avoid lying down. If discomfort, nausea, or vomiting occurs during initial meals, return to liquid diet and try again after two days.

Solid and liquid foods (water, tea) must be separated by 20–30 minutes to prevent gastric distension.

● Drink plenty of fluids to avoid dehydration and constipation.

● You may appropriately supplement with protein powder, immune boosters, or commercial formulations.

● Unsweetened teas and black coffee may be consumed in moderation, depending on individual tolerance.

☑ ✗ Avoid overly tough or fibrous meats.

☑ Avoid high-fiber vegetables and fruits with stems, stalks, or seeds.

Avoid gas-producing foods: sweet potato, winter noodles, onions, leeks.

Avoid oily soups, ice cream, flavored milk, pudding, and sugary drinks.
