## Food Red, Yellow, Green Light Chart

★ Green light: Fresh, natural, and oil- or sugar-free foods that can be consumed daily.

★ Yellow light: Processed, refined foods with minimal added oil or sugar; consume 1–2 times per week.

★ Red light: Highly processed, refined, high in oil and sugar foods; avoid as much as possible during weight loss periods.

| Major Food Categories | Green Light Foods | Yellow Light Foods | Red Light Foods |
|------------------------|-------------------|--------------------|-----------------|
| Grains and root/stem vegetables | Rice, noodles, boiled corn, potatoes, yam, pumpkin, sweet potato | Bowknot rice cake, bread, radish cake, stir-fried noodles, fried rice, pan-fried buns | Fried rice, baked bread, french fries, cake, glutinous rice cake, potato chips, donuts, mooncakes, instant noodles, popcorn, pancakes, egg rolls, etc. |
| Dairy | Skim milk, low-fat milk, unsweetened zero-fat yogurt or kefir | Whole milk, sweetened yogurt or kefir | Condensed milk, flavored milk, ice cream, yogurt drink |
| Seafood, poultry, and meat | Lightly fried, steamed, boiled, stewed, pickled, roasted, or cold-salad lean meat with skin or fat removed | Tuna canned in water, salted fish, fish balls | Oil-soaked canned fish, fried fish products, fish maw, sausages, fatty meat, large intestine, bacon, ham, dried meat, three-layer meat, sweet and spicy, meat flakes, etc. |
| Eggs | Steamed eggs, boiled eggs, poached eggs, tea eggs, lightly fried eggs | Three-color eggs, preserved eggs, salted eggs | — |
| Legumes | Unsweetened or low-sugar soy milk, tofu, pickled dried beans, bean sheets | Sweet soy milk, sweet bean curd, stinky tofu, fried bean skin, wheat gluten, fried tofu |
| Vegetables | Steamed, cold-salad, or lightly-oiled cooked vegetables | Pickled vegetables, canned vegetables, canned vegetable juice | Fried eggplant, fried mushrooms, fried vegetables |
| Fruits | Various fresh fruits, fresh fruit juice without sugar | Canned fruit juice with sugar | Fruit salad, pie, avocado, coconut, lychee, rambutan, preserved fruits, etc. |
| Oils | Various plant oils | Peanuts, cashews, sunflower seeds, almonds, pistachios | Salad dressing, Thousand Island dressing, peanut butter, cream, lard |
| Beverages | Plain water, unsweetened tea and coffee | — | Soda, cola, Sprite, undiluted sports drinks, cocoa, etc. |
| Seasonings | White vinegar, scallions, ginger, salt, light soy sauce | Black pepper, five-spice powder, mustard, soy sauce paste | Sugar, ketchup, satay sauce, oyster sauce, mayonnaise, jam, fruit sugar, honey |
| Desserts and snacks | Low-salt seaweed, rice crackers | Soda crackers | Chocolate, pastry, small pastries, wedding cakes, donuts, pancakes, red bean paste cakes, pudding, sufu, fish-flavored silk, etc. |

※ After one week of post-surgical weight-loss diet, once clear liquid diet is completed, avoid drinking meat broth, oily broths, and sports drinks.
