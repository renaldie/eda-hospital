Prevention of falls exercise for patients at medium-risk of falling一、Exercise objectives: Reduce fall risk, enhance strength, and improve body mobility.

二、Exercise prescription: Perform 15 repetitions per session, four sets per day.

三、Precautions: (1) This health education sheet provides general guidelines only; individual conditions may vary.  
(2) For personalized exercise recommendations, please consult the Rehabilitation Department of this hospital.

• Gluteal lift exercise: Enhances trunk stability, strengthens core muscles, and improves lower limb strength.

• Hand-pedal exercise: Increases hand mobility, dexterity, and strength.

<div style="text-align: center;"><img src="imgs/img_in_image_box_24_679_410_889.jpg" alt="Image" width="16%" /></div>

Bending both legs and pressing feet against the bed.

<div style="text-align: center;"><img src="imgs/img_in_image_box_427_677_781_884.jpg" alt="Image" width="14%" /></div>

Raise the buttocks, maintaining a straight line from shoulders to hips to knees.

<div style="text-align: center;"><img src="imgs/img_in_image_box_797_675_1169_884.jpg" alt="Image" width="15%" /></div>

Lower the buttocks.

<div style="text-align: center;"><img src="imgs/img_in_image_box_1284_587_1563_939.jpg" alt="Image" width="11%" /></div>

Place both feet on the ground, and raise both hands into a fist to shoulder height.

<div style="text-align: center;"><img src="imgs/img_in_image_box_1655_587_1940_939.jpg" alt="Image" width="11%" /></div>

Make circular motions forward with both hands, starting with the right hand.

<div style="text-align: center;"><img src="imgs/img_in_image_box_2044_584_2304_940.jpg" alt="Image" width="10%" /></div>

Then switch to the left hand and make forward circular motions.

● Leg lift exercise: Enhances quadriceps strength, trunk stability, and knee joint flexibility.

<div style="text-align: center;"><img src="imgs/img_in_image_box_82_1199_357_1528.jpg" alt="Image" width="11%" /></div>

Place both feet on the ground and hold onto the bed rail.

<div style="text-align: center;"><img src="imgs/img_in_image_box_464_1200_705_1534.jpg" alt="Image" width="10%" /></div>

Lift the right leg forward, extend the knee, hold for 5 seconds, then relax and lower the leg.

<div style="text-align: center;"><img src="imgs/img_in_image_box_807_1199_1055_1533.jpg" alt="Image" width="10%" /></div>

Lift the left leg forward, extend the knee, hold for 5 seconds, then relax and lower the leg.

• Toe raise exercise: Increases ankle mobility and calf strength, and improves balance and sensitivity.

<div style="text-align: center;"><img src="imgs/img_in_image_box_1288_1200_1548_1538.jpg" alt="Image" width="10%" /></div>

Hold onto the walking frame with both hands, and stand up only after ensuring no dizziness.

<div style="text-align: center;"><img src="imgs/img_in_image_box_1649_1198_1919_1545.jpg" alt="Image" width="11%" /></div>

Hold onto the walking frame with both hands, and lift the toes of both feet.

<div style="text-align: center;"><img src="imgs/img_in_image_box_2018_1199_2300_1542.jpg" alt="Image" width="11%" /></div>

Relax both feet back to the ground, and pay attention to safety to avoid falling.
