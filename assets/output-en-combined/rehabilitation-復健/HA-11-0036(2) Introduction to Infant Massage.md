## Understanding Infant and Toddler Massage

## Purpose of Massage

Promote neurological development and motor skills in young children

Improve infant sleep quality

Increase newborn weight

Reduce infant colic (gas pain)

Reduce caregiver stress and anxiety

Enhance attachment and intimacy between parent and child

## Precautions

Perform massage when the baby is calm and awake

Conduct massage in a softly lit, quiet environment

Massage providers should avoid wearing jewelry or watches during massage

Stop immediately if the baby cries or shows discomfort during the session

## Frequency: 1–3 times per day, each session lasting about 5–15 minutes

### 1. Legs

<div style="text-align: center;"><img src="imgs/img_in_image_box_62_568_263_851.jpg" alt="Image" width="16%" /></div>

Start:

Glide from the thigh down to the ankle

Foot sole:

Gently push from heel to toe

Toes:

Gently rub each toe

Dorsum of foot:

Gently push from toes toward the ankle

Finish:

Glide from ankle up to the thigh

### 2. Abdomen

<div style="text-align: center;"><img src="imgs/img_in_image_box_40_1148_244_1418.jpg" alt="Image" width="17%" /></div>

Start:

Gently press down with both hands on the abdomen

Abdomen:

Alternate hands, sliding in a waterwheel motion

Bend knees:

Bring the baby's knees up and toward the abdomen

Sun and Moon Massage:

Alternate hands, drawing clockwise sun and moon patterns  
(left sun, right moon)

Bend knees:

Bring the baby's knees up and toward the abdomen

### 3. Hands

<div style="text-align: center;"><img src="imgs/img_in_image_box_52_121_252_376.jpg" alt="Image" width="16%" /></div>

Start:
Slide from arm to wrist

Palms:
Gently push from wrist toward fingertips

Fingers:
Gently rub each finger

Back of hands:
Gently push from wrist toward fingertips

End:
Slide from wrist to arm

### 4. Back

<div style="text-align: center;"><img src="imgs/img_in_image_box_38_660_304_904.jpg" alt="Image" width="22%" /></div>

Back rubbing:
Use palms to gently rub the baby's back, moving from head to buttocks

Full-body rubbing:
Gently rub the baby's back with palms to the heels of both feet

Circular massage:
Use the pads of the four fingers to draw small circles on the baby's back

Comb-style massage:
Form hands into a comb shape, massaging from back to buttocks
