## Children's Headache Precautions

1. After returning home, rest in bed as much as possible, elevate the head of the bed, ensure adequate sleep, and take medication as instructed by your doctor.

2. Try to reduce daily stress and relax your mind.

3. Seek follow-up care if any of the following symptoms occur: blurred vision or double vision, weakness or numbness on one side of the body, tingling or pain, neck stiffness, persistent vomiting, or confusion. Headaches can have many causes; it is recommended to return to the outpatient clinic for further evaluation.
