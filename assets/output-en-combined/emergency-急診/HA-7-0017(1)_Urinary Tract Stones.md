## Urinary Tract Stones

1. Sudden, severe, sharp pain originating in the back and radiating to the sides; in males, it extends down to the testicles; in females, it reaches the bladder.

2. When pain is severe, nausea, vomiting, pallor, and cold sweats may occur. Frequent urination or difficulty in urination may also appear.
