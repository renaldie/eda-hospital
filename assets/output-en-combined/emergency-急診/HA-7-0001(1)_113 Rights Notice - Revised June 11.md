## Information for Victims of Domestic Violence

When you suffer domestic violence, you may feel afraid, helpless, sad, and unsure of what to do. You can call the 24-hour free hotline at 113, the Domestic Violence and Sexual Offense Prevention Center, where trained staff will provide you with necessary assistance and counseling. Your personal information and the content of your request will be kept strictly confidential.

### 2. Assistance Provided by Various Social Welfare Centers

| Situation | Assistance Provided |
|---------|---------------------|
| If there are safety concerns and no family or friends available for support | Can arrange emergency shelter. |
| Unable to afford medical treatment fees | Request medical institutions to assist with billing. |
| Individuals facing economic difficulties | Apply for legal litigation fee subsidies and emergency living assistance. |
| Individuals who need employment to maintain livelihood | Refer to employment service centers and provide vocational training or employment counseling. |

3. Victims of domestic violence can directly file a report with local police stations. If issues arise, the domestic violence prevention center can refer you to police stations or police bureaus in Kaohsiung to assist with completing the reporting procedures.

### 4. Procedure for Applying for Protection Orders:

| Item | Type of Protection Order | Description |
|------|--------------------------|-------------|
| One | Emergency Protection Order | No formal hearing is required. After receiving the application, the court will issue an emergency temporary protection order within four hours, unless there are legitimate reasons. |
| Two | Temporary Protection Order | The court may issue a general temporary protection order without a hearing or before the hearing concludes. |
| Three | Regular Protection Order | Issued after a court hearing. |

$ ^{*} $ The validity period of a regular protection order is up to two years, effective from the date of issuance.

### 5. Assistance Provided by Medical Institutions

| Service | Description |
|--------|-------------|
| Injury Examination and Treatment | Hospitals cannot refuse treatment or injury examination without just cause. If needed, social workers or nurses from the hospital can accompany you, respect your privacy, and assist in ensuring your safety. |
| Issuance of Medical Diagnosis Certificate | This serves as one of the evidences for initiating legal proceedings. Even if you have not yet decided to file a lawsuit, it is recommended to have a doctor issue a diagnosis certificate for future reference. When you report to the police, the police will use this document as evidence during the recording process. |

$ ^{*} $ If you are concerned that the diagnosis certificate may be discovered by the abuser upon being taken home, it is recommended to have a close relative or friend assist in storing it.

*If you still have questions about your rights, you may contact the social work department of this center (telephone: 6150988).
