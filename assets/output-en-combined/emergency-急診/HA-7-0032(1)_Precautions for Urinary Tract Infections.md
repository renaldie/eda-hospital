## Precautions for Urinary Tract Infections

1. Drink plenty of water; if there are no contraindications, consume at least 2000 ml of water daily.

2. Avoid holding urine and maintain cleanliness of the genital area. Women should wipe from front to back after urination; women should urinate immediately after intercourse, drink plenty of water, and change sanitary pads frequently during menstruation.

3. Eat foods rich in vitamin C, such as oranges, tangerines, tomatoes, and papaya.

4. Take a shower instead of bathing; avoid wearing tight synthetic fiber underwear, socks, or tight pants.

5. If you experience high fever, chills, or flank pain, seek medical attention promptly.

6. Hematuria is a common symptom of cystitis and requires outpatient treatment.
