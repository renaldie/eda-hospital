# Caution for Back Injury

1. Lie flat as much as possible and rest adequately.

2. Avoid bending over or lifting heavy objects.

3. When moving, hold the painful area with your hands to reduce pain.

4. Apply ice for the first two days after injury, which helps reduce swelling and relieve pain.

(1) Place an appropriate amount of ice in a cold pack, filling it with water to one-third capacity  
(2) Invert the cold pack to check for leaks; wrap it in a dry towel and apply it to the injured area

5. Take medications as prescribed and attend regular outpatient follow-up visits.

6. If you experience hematuria, back pain accompanied by cold sweats, please go to the emergency department immediately.
