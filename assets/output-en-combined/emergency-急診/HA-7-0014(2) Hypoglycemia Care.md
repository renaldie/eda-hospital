# Care for Hypoglycemia

## What is hypoglycemia?

Hypoglycemia typically refers to blood glucose levels below 70 mg/dL, which can lead to the symptoms associated with hypoglycemia. Therefore, when blood glucose drops below 70 mg/dL, one should be alert to the possibility of hypoglycemia occurring.

## Why does hypoglycemia occur?

1. Failing to eat after insulin or medication administration as scheduled  
2. Overuse of oral hypoglycemic agents or insulin  
3. Sudden increase in physical activity without appropriate snacks  
4. Prolonged fasting without food intake
