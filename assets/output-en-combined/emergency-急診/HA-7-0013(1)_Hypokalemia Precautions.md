## Precautions for Low Blood Potassium

1. Applicable to: patients with low blood potassium levels

2. Increase intake of potassium-rich foods such as bananas, oranges, tangerines, papayas, dried persimmons, honeydew melon, tomatoes, dark green vegetables, chicken seasoning, and meat broth.

3. If you continue to experience diarrhea or vomiting, or if your symptoms do not improve or muscle weakness worsens, seek emergency care.

4. The underlying causes of hypokalemia should be evaluated at an endocrinology or nephrology outpatient clinic to arrange further diagnosis and treatment.
