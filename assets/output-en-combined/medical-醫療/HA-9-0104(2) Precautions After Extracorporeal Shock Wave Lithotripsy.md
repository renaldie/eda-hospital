# Post-Procedure Precautions for Extracorporeal Shock Wave Lithotripsy (ESWL)

1. During the period when small stones are expelled after the procedure, some patients may experience renal colic or mild hematuria for about 2â€“3 days, which can be managed with medication.

2. If patients experience severe renal colic, high fever, or severe hematuria, they should immediately visit the urology outpatient clinic or the emergency department of our hospital for treatment.

3. It is recommended to drink at least 3000 cc of water daily, with an average of 250 cc every two hours.

4. Increase dietary fiber intake: whole grain bread, oatmeal, fruits.

5. Suitable beverages: low-sodium mineral water, fruit tea, diluted juice, lemon juice, small amounts of milk and cheese.

6. Less suitable beverages: black tea, coffee, soda, alcoholic drinks, beer.

7. Avoid obesity.

8. Exercise: going up and down stairs, jumping rope, jogging.

9. Avoid sweating or any form of fluid loss.

10. Avoid stress.

11. Ensure adequate sleep.

12. Foods to reduce for different types of stones:

| Stone Type           | Foods to Avoid                                                                                      |
|----------------------|-----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------|
| Calcium oxalate stones | Black tea, coconuts, coffee, soda, beer, red berry juice, broad beans, spinach, apples, tomatoes, leeks, soybeans, beets, okra, sweet potatoes, figs, plums, cherries, almonds, chocolate, cocoa, peanuts, cabbage, celery, asparagus |
| Uric acid stones      | Sea fish (small dried fish, sardines), internal organs (heart, liver, kidneys, brain, spleen, intestines), meat juice, meat broth, sausages, shiitake mushrooms, button mushrooms, asparagus, legumes, yeast (yogurt, acidophilus, Jianshu sugar), monosodium glutamate (MSG), abalone, clams, shrimp, mussels, sprouted legumes, seaweed |
| Calcium phosphate stones | Yeast, wheat, germ, shrimp, shiitake mushrooms, cereal flakes, internal organs, egg yolk, milk, legumes, whole grains, nuts, cocoa powder, chocolate, juice powder |
