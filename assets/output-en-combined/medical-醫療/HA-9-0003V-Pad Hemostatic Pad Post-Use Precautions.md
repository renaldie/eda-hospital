## V -Pad Hemostatic Tablet Post-Use Precautions:

$ ^{※} $ After removing the catheter and applying the hemostatic tablet, apply sandbag compression and remain flat on your back for 2 hours before getting up to move around. The hemostatic cotton can be removed after 24 hours.

※ Always monitor the puncture site for any signs of bleeding or hematoma. If any abnormalities are observed, contact your doctor immediately.

※ When the patient coughs, stretches, or lifts their legs, they should gently press on the wound with appropriate force themselves.

$ ^{※} $ Bathing or showering is allowed within two days; after two days, bathing is permitted.

※ For three days after hemostasis is complete, avoid strenuous exercise and do not lift heavy objects.
