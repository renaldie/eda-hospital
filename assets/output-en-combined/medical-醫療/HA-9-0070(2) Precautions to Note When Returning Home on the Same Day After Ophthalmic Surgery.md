# Post-Operative Care Instructions for Eye Surgery

## 1. Definition

Patients can return home on the day of eye surgery. To protect the surgical eye and prevent infection, you need to follow the care instructions below.

## 2. Applicable Population

Patients who have undergone eye surgery

## 3. Care Instructions (as follows)

<div style="text-align: center;"><img src="imgs/img_in_image_box_2_559_273_831.jpg" alt="Image" width="22%" /></div>


☐ Do not remove the gauze on the day of surgery, and do not administer medication.

☐ The eye drops and ointment received on the day of surgery should not be opened. Please bring all of them to your follow-up appointment the next day. We will teach you how to administer the medication properly.

☐ Take the oral medication as instructed on the medication packet.

## 4. Important Notes

1. If the non-surgical eye experiences pain, it may be due to the disinfectant solution entering the eye during surgery. You may use the eye drops or ointment previously obtained during your outpatient visit to alleviate discomfort.

2. After returning home, try to avoid excessive coughing or lifting heavy objects. There are no special restrictions on daily life or diet.

3. If you experience severe vomiting or unbearable pain after returning home, please call the ophthalmology department.

4. You will be scheduled for a follow-up appointment (the day after surgery). Please bring your health insurance card and the eye drops and ointment received on the day of surgery to the ophthalmology outpatient clinic on ___ year ___ month ___ day,星期 ___ , ___ PM ___ o'clock.
