## Semen Analysis Collection and Education Form

One. Testing items: Semen analysis

Two. Pre-collection precautions: Abstinence for 2–7 days prior to collection.

## Three. Collection procedure:

1. Collect all semen in one session using masturbation, without using a condom, into a 40 ml measuring cup.

2. Fill out the return form:

| Is the specimen from a sterilized sample? ☐ Sterilized; ☐ Not sterilized |
| Abstinence duration: ☐ <2 days; ☐ 2–7 days; ☐ >7 days |
| Collection method: ☐ Masturbation; ☐ Other (please specify): |
| Collection volume: ☐ Entire volume of semen; ☐ Approximately ___ ml was missed |
| Collection time: ___ month ___ day ___ hour ___ minute |

## Four. Post-collection precautions:

1. Store the specimen at room temperature; do not refrigerate or heat it. Deliver the specimen and return form to the laboratory reception window within 30 minutes.
