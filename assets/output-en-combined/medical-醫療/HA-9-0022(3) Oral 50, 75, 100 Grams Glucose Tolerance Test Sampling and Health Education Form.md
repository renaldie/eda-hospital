# Instruction for 50/75/100 gram Oral Glucose Tolerance Test

## NOTE:

### 1. 50-gram oral glucose tolerance test :

1.1 No need of fasting.

1.2 Come to the phlebotomy room with the examination sheet. Receive 5 bottles of glucose solution (Concentration: 50%, 20ml per bottle).

1.3 Drink all 5 bottles of glucose solution within 5 minutes and then DO NOT eat anything. (Drink water is acceptable)

1.4 You will have a blood draw at the 60 mins mark.

### 2. 75-gram oral glucose tolerance test :

2.1 Fasting for 8hrs. You will have a blood draw first to measure fasting blood glucose.

2.2 Come to the phlebotomy room with the examination sheet. Receive 8 bottles of glucose solution (Concentration: 50%, 20ml per bottle).

2.3 Drink 7 and half bottles of glucose solution within 5 minutes and then DO NOT eat anything. (Drink water is acceptable)

2.4 Come to the counter and our staffs will write down the specific time for blood draw after drinking of glucose solution.

2.5 You will have a blood draw at the 30, 60, 90 and 120 mins mark.

### 3. 100-gram oral glucose tolerance test :

3.1 Fasting for 8hrs. You will have a blood draw first to measure fasting blood glucose.

3.2 Come to the phlebotomy room with the examination sheet. Receive 10 bottles of glucose solution (Concentration: 50%, 20ml per bottle).

3.3 Drink all 10 bottles of glucose solution within 5 minutes and then DO NOT eat anything. (Drink water is acceptable)

3.4 Come to the counter and our staffs will write down the specific time for blood draw after drinking of glucose solution.

3.5 You will have a blood draw at the 30, 60, 120 and 180 mins mark.
