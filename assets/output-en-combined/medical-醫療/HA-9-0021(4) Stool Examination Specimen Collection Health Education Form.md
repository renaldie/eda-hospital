## III. Collection Procedure:

1. Feces should be collected in a squat toilet, toilet bowl, clean trash bag, or on paper, without mixing urine, toilet water, or toilet paper to avoid contamination.

2. Open the bottle cap and use a plastic stick to scoop feces. Select feces containing blood, pus, or mucus and place it into the bottle, then tightly seal it.

3. For watery or loose stools, use a plastic syringe to draw approximately 2 c.c. of feces and place it into the bottle.

## IV. Post-collection Notes:

1. Routine stool examination (Stool routine) and occult blood test [chemical method] (OB [Chemical]): The specimen should be delivered to the laboratory within 4 hours at room temperature. If delivery cannot be made on the same day, the specimen should be refrigerated and delivered within 24 hours.

2. Parasite ova concentration method (Parasite OVA (Concentrated method)): The specimen should be delivered to the laboratory at room temperature within 1 hour after collection.
