## Low-Fat Diet Health Education

1. Target Audience: High cholesterol, cholecystitis, gallstones, severe diarrhea, cardiovascular diseases.

2. Purpose: Limit fat intake in the diet to reduce the burden on the pancreas, intestines, gallbladder, and cardiovascular system.

3. Food Categories: Skim milk, lean meat, liver, heart, kidney, non-fried soy products, fish roe, oysters, shrimp, eel, abalone, octopus, sardines, scallops, squid...

4. Cooking methods such as steaming, boiling, stir-frying, stewing, roasting, braising, and cold salad can be used to enhance food texture without adding oil, and non-irritating seasonings can be utilized to stimulate appetite.
